Dear Sky, When I think of you, I think of so many things. How amazing you are, how strong you are, body, soul, and mind. I think of how much brighter you make my life. Why so many bears… right?! I’ll answer your question. I was doing my research and found out that a bear might just be your spirit animal. The bear has family loyalty - you are devoted to your family and prioritize your loved ones above all. Even above yourself, that is a selfless trait that not many people embody, but you do… and it’s beautiful. The bear has the ability to endure through life’s harsh seasons. You have had a number of hurdles and bullets fired at you (literally and figuratively) , but no matter how big the setback that life has thrown your way, you have managed to show it who is boss and raise above it, put your crown back on and keep on moving despite how hard it might be everyday. I am surprised at how you do it.. but still very proud. The bear is a representation of leadership. You take charge of situations and you do it well. You do it even if you don’t necessarily have the strength to, but you push yourself with the hope that it will bear good fruit. I know you put in a lot of work into what you deem as important. I want you to know that I see it and I recognize your hardwork and ambition. I hope this new era of your life brings you nothing but happiness and joy, hearty laughter and beautiful memories. May all the dreams you have, that you keep in the most secret corner of your heart and mind be realized by the universe and come true. You deserve it. Yours truly, Lois.
